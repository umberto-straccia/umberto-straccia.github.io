  Registration

All deadlines are 23:59 UK Time

Author registration / early bird registration deadline: July 28 2014

Registration fee on or before July 28: 285 GBP (this includes proceedings/conference pack, lunch, coffee breaks, and banquet on September 16 at Oriel College).

Registration on or after July 29 and until August 31: 335 GBP.

Extra banquet tickets: 70 GBP per person.

Accommodation at conference venue (St. Anne's College) will be available to book together with registration at 75 GBP per night (en suite bathroom, with breakfast) ONLY UNTIL JULY 28 -- after that, rooms will not be held and attendees must book on their own.

Finally, we will offer the possibility to book a tour of the Bodleian Library on a first come, first served basis (maximum group size is 26). Attendees will be able to buy tickets at an additional 7 GBP per person when registering. The tour will be on Tuesday, September 16.
