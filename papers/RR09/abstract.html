A Minimal Deductive System for General Fuzzy RDF

In Proceedings of the 3rd International Conference on Web Reasoning and Rule Systems (RR-09).

Abstract:
It is well-known that crisp RDF is not suitable to represent vague information. Fuzzy RDF variants are emerging to overcome this limitations.

In this work we provide, under a very general semantics, a deductive system for a salient fragment of fuzzy RDF. We then also show how we may compute the top-k answers of the union of conjunctive queries in which answers may be scored by means of a scoring function.
