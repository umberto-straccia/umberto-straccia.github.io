A Personalized Collaborative Digital Library Environment

In Proceeedings of the 5th International Conference on Asian Digital Libraries (ICADL-02), 2002.

Abstract:
We envisage a Digital Library not only as an information resource where users may submit queries to satisfy their information need, but also as a collaborative working and meeting space. We will present a personalized collaborative Digital Library environment, where users may organise the information space according to their own subjective view, may become aware of each other, exchange information and knowledge with each other, may build communities and may get recommendations based on preference patterns of the users.
